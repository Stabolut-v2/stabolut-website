# Stabolut — The Stablecoin Standard

Stabolut is building a new generation of stablecoins: **multi-currency, yield-bearing, and fully decentralized.** 

Unlike USDT or USDC that sit idle in your wallet, USB puts your capital to work—backed by Bitcoin and Ether, hedged with delta-neutral strategies, and earning sustainable yield through Aave lending pools.

## The Problem We Solve

Current stablecoin options force a tradeoff:

| | USDT/USDC | DAI/sDAI | Ethena USDe | **Stabolut USB** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Yield** | ❌ None | ✅ Yes (SSR) | ✅ Yes (funding) | ✅ Yes (Aave + sUSB) |
| **Decentralized** | ❌ Centralized | ✅ DAI yes | ⚠️ Custodied | ✅ Self-custody |
| **Backing** | Fiat/bonds | Crypto | ETH/BTC delta-neutral | BTC/ETH + Aave |
| **Transparency** | Audited | On-chain | OES | On-chain PoR |

**USB combines the best of both worlds:** the yield of Ethena with the decentralization of DAI, backed by the most liquid cryptoassets.

## How It Works (Simple)

```
1. Deposit USDC/USDT → USBVault
2. Vault supplies to Aave (earns lending yield)
3. You receive USB (1:1 pegged)
4. Wrap USB → sUSB to earn yield automatically
```

No lockups. No complex strategies. Just deposit, mint, earn.

## Key Differentiators

- **🪙 Multi-currency:** USB isn't just USD—architecture supports multiple fiat-pegged stablecoins
- **🔐 Self-custody:** Funds stay in smart contracts, not with custodians
- **📈 Sustainable yield:** Aave lending rates, not speculative funding
- **🔍 Proof of Reserves:** Real-time on-chain attestation
- **🌐 Arbitrum native:** Low fees, fast finality
