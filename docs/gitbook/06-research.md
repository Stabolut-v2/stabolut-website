## Why Delta-Neutral?

Delta-neutral strategies allow stablecoins to generate yield without directional market risk:

1. **Long spot + short perpetuals** = neutral position
2. **Funding rate collection** = the yield
3. **Overcollateralization** = safety buffer

Stabolut's approach uses **Aave lending** as the yield source instead of funding rates, providing more predictable and sustainable returns.

## Regulation Watch

The **GENIUS Act** and similar frameworks are shaping how stablecoins will be regulated in the US and EU. Stabolut is designed with compliance in mind:
- Decentralized architecture (not a custodial issuer)
- Transparent proof of reserves
- Built-in geo-blocking capabilities (US-restricted markets)
- Multi-jurisdiction legal structure
