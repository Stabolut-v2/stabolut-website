# Compliance & Legal

## Multi-Jurisdiction Strategy

Stabolut is being built with a global regulatory framework in mind:

- **United States:** Geo-blocked via Cloudflare Workers + backend filtering
- **European Union:** Designed with MiCA principles (transparency, reserves)
- **Asia-Pacific:** Engaging with regulators in friendly jurisdictions

## Proof of Reserves

Transparency is core to Stabolut:

- **Real-time on-chain data:** All reserves visible on Arbitrum
- **Automated attestations:** Smart contract-based reporting
- **Third-party audits:** Regular security and reserve audits

## Risk Disclosures

- Smart contract risk (audited but not infallible)
- Aave protocol risk (underlying lending market)
- Oracle risk (price feed dependencies)
- Liquidity risk (redemption delays during high volatility)
- Regulatory risk (evolving stablecoin frameworks)

> USB is not a security. sUSB is a yield-bearing wrapper, not an investment contract.
