# Testnet — Try Stabolut Now

The Stabolut testnet is live on **Arbitrum Sepolia**. You can mint USB, stake sUSB, and earn yield—all with test funds.

## Step 1: Get Testnet USDC

Join the **Stabolut Telegram group** and use the faucet bot:

1. Open Telegram and join the Stabolut beta group
2. Post your EVM wallet address (0x...)
3. The bot sends you **0.005 ETH** (for gas) + **5 USDC** (to mint USB)

> Each wallet can be funded once. You can also get USDC from the Aave Sepolia faucet directly at app.aave.com

## Step 2: Connect to testnet.stabolut.com

1. Go to **testnet.stabolut.com**
2. Connect your MetaMask wallet
3. Switch to **Arbitrum Sepolia** network (chain ID: 421614)
4. You'll see your USDC and USB balances

## Step 3: Mint USB

1. Navigate to the **Mint** page
2. Enter the amount of USDC to deposit
3. Approve USDC spending (one-time)
4. Click **Mint USB**
5. Receive USB 1:1 in your wallet

## Step 4: Earn Yield with sUSB

1. Go to the **Stake** page
2. Enter the amount of USB to wrap
3. Click **Wrap** → receive sUSB
4. sUSB automatically earns yield from Aave

## Step 5: Monitor Your Position

The **Dashboard** shows:
- Your USB/sUSB balances
- Current sUSB APY
- Total Value Locked (TVL)
- Transaction history
- Yield earned over time

## Important Notes

- **Testnet funds have no real value.** Everything resets periodically.
- **sUSB has a 24h cooldown** on testnet before you can unwrap.
- **Maximum supply:** 100,000 USB during testnet phase.
- Found a bug? Report it in the Telegram group.
